    Deluxe Double

    BOOK NOW

    The Deluxe Double room sleeps four guests comfortably with 2 double beds. Oversized windows and 9-foot ceilings give these rooms extra height and lots of natural light. This luxury hotel room features include mid-century black and white dual sconces, an expansive brass-trimmed work desk accompanied with a case study desk chair, a large chest of drawers, an additional storage area, and a Smeg refrigerator fully stocked gourmet minibar.

    ROOM FEATURES
    310 square feet
    2 double beds with 100% Egyptian cotton linens & custom cashmere throws
    Workspace with a custom built-in expansive desk
    Hardwood flooring
    Smeg refrigerator
    Fully stocked gourmet minibar
    Samsung smart TV with streaming
    Nespresso coffee machine
    Tivoli clock radio with Bluetooth
    Views of downtown Manhattan/TriBeCa

    BATH FEATURES
    Five Wits bath products
